Our founder Dapper Dan

Dapper Dans est: 1929

For many years our founder Dapper Dan had a dream, he wanted to ensure a way for his closest friends and family to fit in society. Dapper Dan was born near the begining of World War one in 1915. Growing up in Irland Dan and his family eventualy had to move to America becaues of the potato famine. Eventualy, they found themselvs in New York, with hardly a penny to their name. In order to make a living, Dans mother started a tayloring buisness that specialized in altering suits and dresses and patching up threadbear clothing. Eventualy Dan had grown and taken over his mothers buisness, giving it the aptly named Dapper Dan He decided to expand from his mothers work and hand tailor suits for buisness men. Over time the family buisness was passed on to Dans lineage, to his son Sam, to the current owner of Dapper Dan, Dapper Stan.
