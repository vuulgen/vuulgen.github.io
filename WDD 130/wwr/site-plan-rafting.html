Overview

Purpose

Here at White Water Hazards we personally challenge people to have the fun of their lives. We want to give them an experience that they will never forget, as well as give them bragging rights for making it through our most rigorous corses which is comprised of Hells Canyon and other notable rapids, each with variable difficulty. Much like our company motto "The Fun is Mandatory". To help demonstrate here is the first draft from our advertisement devision: "Here at White Water Hazards we challenge you our greatest thrill, to face the open jaws of Hells Canyon. Get ready for a mandatory fun experience as you float down the most exiting white water rapids this side of the Rocky mountains. Grab a camera, because nobody will believe you or your stories of adventure here at White Water Hazards"

Audience

Thrill seekers and adrenaline junkies. College students and parents who are desperate for thrilling ride. people who consider themselves adventurous and or rebelious.
