Dapper Dan

Overview

Purpose

If you are like me, then you like to look good, to for the occasion, to look sharp and snappy, but sometimes you can't find the right pice to go with your outfit. You may not even know how you could up for the occasion, there are many different events that call for a specific type of wear, in example for black tie affares you are required to have a nice tuxedo with a black tie, but where do you get one? I like you have asked myself this quesiton many times until I got to where I am today! Oh yes I can get you a tuxedo and the tie to boot, but there is so much more... Mens classic wear is hard to find these days, and even if you have had your eyes on somehting from the classic period, it is bound to be outrageously priced, that is why I am here. That's right, I Dan of Dapper Dans will supply you for all your snazzy needs.

Audience

Men who are looking for formal, classic, or vintage wear
